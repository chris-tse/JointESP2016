Abstract Submission

  • Choose a presentation type from “Individual Paper” (25 mins); “Forum” (Multiple short papers on the same theme/topic, 40-60 mins; Abstracts of all papers required): “Workshop” (One or multiple presenters, 40-60 mins). If you do not have a preference, you could choose “Any” and leave the decision to the vetting committee to be either an individual paper or part of a forum.
  • Enter the information of the author(s): the full name, E-mail, affiliation and postal address of the affiliation.
  • Choose a theme for your presentation.
  • Abstracts for Individual Papers and Workshops should be less than 300 words. Titles of these two types of presentation should not exceed 15 words each.
  • Application for the Forum type of presentation should have one generic title (less than 15 words) that explains what the focus of the forum is. This title should be typed into the Title field/box on the submission page. In the abstract field, input the title (less than 10 words) and the abstract (less than 200 words) of each individual presentation in the order of presentation, i.e. Title 1 -> Abstract 1 -> Title 2 -> Abstract 2 -> Title 3 -> Abstract 3 -> Title 4 -> Abstract 4. Please note that the maximum number of individual papers that can be included in a forum is four.
  • Click here to download a sample abstract: Download Sample Abstract


All papers submitted to this conference must be original work and are not submitted to other conferences.
