Call For Presentations

We invite presentations in all aspects of ESP from teachers, practitioners, researchers, research students, and training consultants from the industry.

Conference themes include, but are not limited to:

  1. Curriculum and course design in ESP
  2. Materials design and writing in ESP
  3. Development and application of ESP theories
  4. ESP teaching and learning approaches and methods
  5. Innovations in ESP research and instruction
  6. ESP teacher development
  7. Development of ESP testing and assessment
  8. English teaching and learning for academic purposes
  9. English teaching and learning for professional purposes
  10. English teaching and research for business purposes
  11. English teaching and research for legal purposes
  12. Intercultural communication in ESP teaching

Important Dates:

Abstract submission period: March 18-June 30, 2016 (23:59 JST)
Notification of acceptance: July 1 , 2016 (23:59 JST)

Abstract Submission

  • All presentation application info should be submitted on the conference website submission page alone with the required information of authors.
  • Abstracts for Individual Papers and Workshops should be less than 300 words. Titles of these two types of presentation should not exceed 15 words each.
  • Application for the Forum type of presentation should have one generic title (less than 15 words) that explains what the focus of the forum is. This title should be typed into the Title field/box on the submission page. In the abstract field, input the title (less than 10 words) and the abstract (less than 200 words) of each individual presentation in the order of presentation, i.e. Title 1 -> Abstract 1 -> Title 2 -> Abstract 2 -> Title 3 -> Abstract 3 -> Title 4 -> Abstract 4. Please note that the maximum number of individual papers that can be included in a forum is four.
