Venue and Access

The 3rd International Symposium on ESP will be held starting from August 19, 2016 at the University of Electro-Communications, Tokyo. The campus is located in Chofu City, on the West of Tokyo (20 minutes by train from Shinjuku).

Please refer below for information about the location of UEC Tokyo and transportation to Chofu.

The University of Electro-Communications

UEC Tokyo is located in Chofu, on the West of Tokyo. To access Chofu, use Keio line from Shinjuku rain station. The campus is located 10min by walk from North exit. The conference will be held in the building East-B (Êù±-B). More information about the location on UEC website.

Getting to UEC Tokyo from Narita and Haneda Airport

Bus

The Airport Limousine Bus leaves from both airports (Narita & Haneda) and can go directly to Chofu.

Tickets cost 3,300 JPY from Narita and 1,440 JPY from Haneda.

Tickets can be bought at the desks in airport terminals. Please find more information on the company website:

  • Bus From Narita Airport
  • Bus From Haneda Airport

Train

Narita

There are several train lines leaving from Narita airport to several places in Tokyo. You can find all details on Narita airport website. In order to go to Chofu from Narita airport, you have 2 different routes:

  1. Keisei Skyliner
    • Take the Skyliner to Nippori (Total: 35-40 min, 2,470 JPY) (Recommended)
    • Change to the JR Yamanote line to Shinjuku (20 min, 194 JPY)
    • Change for Keio line (Special Express, Express or Semi-Express) going to Keio-Hachioji, Takaosan-guchi or Hashimoto (20-30 min, 237 JPY)
  2. Narita Express
    • Take the Narita Express to Shinjuku (Total: 90 min, 3,190 JPY)
    • Change to the Keio line (Special Express, Express or Semi-Express) going to Keio-Hachioji, Takaosan-guchi or Hashimoto (20-30 min, 237 JPY)

Haneda

The easiest and fastest way to go from Haneda airport is to use Keikyu line (Total: 60 min, 838 JPY):

  • Take the Keikyu line from Haneda to Shinagawa (possible change in Keikyu-Kamata) (20 min, 407 JPY)
  • Change to the JR Yamanote line to Shinjuku (20 min, 194 JPY)
  • Change to the Keio line (Special Express, Express or Semi-Express) going to Keio-Hachioji, Takaosan-guchi or Hashimoto (20-30 min, 237 JPY)

You can find more details on the Haneda Airport website.
