Themes of the 2016 International ESP Conference

Conference themes include, but are not limited to:

  1. Curriculum and course design in ESP
  2. Materials design and writing in ESP
  3. Development and application of ESP theories
  4. ESP teaching and learning approaches and methods
  5. Innovations in ESP research and instruction
  6. ESP teacher development
  7. Development of ESP testing and assessment
  8. English teaching and learning for academic purposes
  9. English teaching and learning for vocational purposes
  10. English teaching and research for business purposes
  11. English teaching and research for legal purposes
  12. Intercultural communication in ESP teaching
  13. Corpus linguistics for ESP education
